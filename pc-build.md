# High-Performance AI & Gaming PC Build

## Overview

Designed and assembled a custom high-performance workstation focused on gaming, AI-assisted workflows, and GPU-intensive applications.

The project involved component research, hardware compatibility planning, thermal management considerations, storage optimization, and long-term upgrade flexibility.

---

## Focus Areas

- GPU performance considerations
- Thermal and airflow planning
- NVMe storage configuration
- Memory optimization
- Power delivery and efficiency

---

## Selected Components

- AMD Ryzen 9 7950X3D
- ASUS ROG Strix X670E-E Gaming WiFi
- NVIDIA RTX 4070 / AI-capable GPU platform
- Samsung 990 PRO NVMe SSDs
- 64GB DDR5 RAM
- be quiet! Dark Base Pro 901
- Kraken Elite 360 RGB Cooler

---

## Technologies & Concepts

- PC hardware architecture
- GPU compute considerations
- Thermal management
- Power optimization
- Storage architecture
- Performance tuning
- Hardware compatibility validation

---

## Outcome

This project strengthened my understanding of modern hardware platforms, GPU-focused workflows, system optimization, and technical troubleshooting within high-performance computing environments.
